Research Centres

A research institute, research centre, research center or research organization is an establishment founded for doing research is a research centre.Research institutes may specialize in basic research or may be oriented to applied research.

Types of Reasearch centres -

Animal Research Centre

- There primary function is to assist investigation in obligation to plan and conduct animal experiments in accord with the highest scietific, humane and ethical principles
For eg - ILS(Institute of Life Sciences)

Medicine Research Centre

- They are also known experimental medicine, encompasses of wide array of research starting from it's basic research.It involves mainly people who are assosscited with clinical trials/research.
For eg - ICMR(Indian Council of Medical Research)

Soil Research Centre

- It is an international journal for publishing research about fundamental and applied aspects of soil science
For eg - ICAR(Indian Council of Agricultural Research)
