Experiments

 A scientific procedure undertaken to make a discovery, test a hypothesis, or demonstrate a known fact.

Some fun experiments to try

Experiment 1 - Floating egg

Step 1 - Take two glasses of water
Step 2 - Add salt to one glass
Step 3 - Now put 1 egg in each glass
Observation - You will observe that the egg in the glass with salt water floats while the other egg sinks

Experiment 2 - Slow fall

 Step 1 - Take two hollow iron rods
Step 2 - Take two balls one magnetic and one iron ball
Step 3 - Drop the balls in the iron rods
Observation - You will observe that the magnetic ball falls slowler than the iron ball
